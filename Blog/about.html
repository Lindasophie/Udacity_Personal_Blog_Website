ABOUT ME

I am Linda, a strong believer in the idea that adventures can be found anywhere.

I travel to destinations with a story to tell, seeking hidden gems, local specialities, off-the-beaten-track experiences, and jaw-dropping natural scenery. I am drawn to the historical and political context that has shaped the lives of people and framed their collective culture.

My goal is to build a community of independent travellers seeking to unlock richer travel experiences; helping the curious discover less-visited places in popular destinations, and rewarding adventures in more remote locations. In doing so, I hope to make travel more sustainable, accessible, and a whole lot of fun.
